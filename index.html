Traffic Management System

The "Traffic Management System" project revolutionizes traffic management by harnessing camera technology to reduce the exhaustive efforts of traffic police officers. This innovative solution utilizes advanced cameras with computer vision capabilities to monitor road conditions, detect traffic violations, and analyze real-time traffic patterns.

Equipped with machine learning algorithms, the system automatically recognizes and categorizes vehicles, captures license plate information, and identifies traffic infractions like red light running and illegal parking. By replacing manual enforcement, it alleviates the burden on traffic police officers, allowing them to focus on other crucial tasks.

Alcohol Detection and Ignition Blockage

The "Alcohol Detection and Ignition Blockage" project aims to enhance road safety by detecting alcohol impairment in drivers and preventing them from starting their vehicles. It incorporates a sensitive alcohol detection sensor within the vehicle, accurately measuring alcohol content in the driver's breath. When alcohol levels exceed the legal limit, the system triggers an immediate response.

To ensure sober driving, an ignition blockage mechanism is integrated. It immobilizes the vehicle's engine by disabling the ignition system upon detecting alcohol impairment. This prevents intoxicated individuals from operating their vehicles, significantly reducing the risk of alcohol-related accidents and fatalities.

The project promotes responsible driving habits and a safer road environment. By combining alcohol detection technology with an ignition blockage mechanism, it aims to save lives, prevent accidents, and encourage responsible alcohol consumption and driving.

Blood Donation Website (Beta)

I've been leading a project called Blood Donors Nepal. The primary goal of this project is to digitalize the blood donation system in Nepal. People who are in need of a specific blood group may not be able to find it in blood banks, so they post it on Facebook, where they have a slim chance of getting what they want. The situation is heartbreaking, and several people have lost their lives as a result of it. I began working on this project with the goal of lowering the number of deaths caused by a lack of blood at the appropriate time. It will create a direct connection between blood donors and those in need of blood. When people keep their blood requests, people with the same blood group or a matching blood group are notified. As a result, once the donor accepts the request, the patient will be able to contact the donor via the website or by sharing contact information. I will not monetize this website and will continue to work on it until it is fully automated. I'm also planning to contact blood banks across the country.

I plan to collaborate with Hamro LifeBank, and Blood Donors Association Nepal, and get more users to come and register on this website. This app already has a prototype and I am working on publishing it.
